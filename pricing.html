Lydia Hutchison

Head of Bus Dev, Acryl Data
You can't expect outbound to work if you can't get any inbounds coming in.

Nailing inbound before spinning up outbound is a critical step most startups miss. It's pretty important in helping you uncover/refine your ICP, personas, the value of your offer and your position in the market.
The goal should be starting with a bus dev or sales dev 'operator' (someone who can strategically prospect at scale while they build and refine process), nail your inbound capture and systems for actioning those leads, then take the messaging and approach that worked with the types of people who it worked with, and flip it outbound using signals and intent.
Capturing inbound/demand is so much easier with AI and the many tools available today. Some of my favorites right now are RevenueHero, ServiceBell, CommonRoom and good old HubSpot!
Nail it before you scale it my friends.
